Minecraft Mob Vote 2020

In September of 2020, Mojang announced a vote for a new mob to be included in Minecraft's upcoming Caves and Cliffs update. Mojang prepared three video trailers that introduced the new creatures. Click on the mob's name to see its video, and hover on the image to reveal who won!

Moobloom

The Moobloom is a friendly mob that would be found in flower forests. They would interact with bees and leave a trail of yellow flowers as they walk. The introduction of this mob would include a new flower: the buttercup!

Moobloom recieves 28.3% of the total vote in the first round and is sadly eliminated.

Iceologer

The Iceologer is a hostile enemy that would spawn in snowy biomes. They are a variation of Illager mobs that attack players and villages. They would summon clouds of ice in the air that they would throw at the player.

Iceologer gets 34.8% of the total vote in the first round, nearly tied with Glow Squid. Mojang holds another tiebreaker vote.

Glow Squid

The Glow Squid is a passive creature that would live deep in the ocean. They are just like normal Minecraft squids with the exception of their glow. Wildly popular Minecraft Youtuber, Dream, encouraged his subscribers to vote for this mob.

Glow Squid recieves 52.7% of the tiebreaker vote, securing its spot as the mob that will be added to the game!
